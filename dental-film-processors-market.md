<p><p>The dental film processors market is divided into two main types: intraoral film processors and extraoral film processors. Intraoral film processors are designed for processing films taken inside the mouth, offering high precision for detailed dental diagnostics. Extraoral film processors cater to films taken outside the mouth, such as panoramic or cephalometric radiographs, enabling wider coverage and more comprehensive imaging. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing diagnostic accuracy and improving patient care in dental practices.</p></p>

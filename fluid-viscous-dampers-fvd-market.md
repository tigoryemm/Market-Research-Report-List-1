<p><p>Fluid Viscous Dampers (FVD) are devices used to absorb vibrations and reduce structural movement in engineering applications. The market for FVD can be classified into two main types: linear and non-linear. Linear dampers provide a constant resistance regardless of the deformation rate, ensuring predictable performance. In contrast, non-linear dampers exhibit varying resistance depending on the applied forces, enabling enhanced adaptability to dynamic conditions. Both types are essential for improving structural safety and comfort in buildings and bridges.</p></p>

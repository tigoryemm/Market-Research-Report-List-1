<p><strong>The Milk Protein Analyzer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ultrasonic Milk Analyzer</li><li>Infrared Milk Analyzer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The milk protein analyzer market includes two primary types: ultrasonic milk analyzers and infrared milk analyzers. Ultrasonic milk analyzers utilize sound waves to assess protein content by measuring changes in acoustic properties. In contrast, infrared milk analyzers use infrared light to identify and quantify various components, including proteins, fats, and lactose, based on absorbance patterns. Both technologies are valuable for ensuring milk quality and composition, aiding in the dairy industry’s efforts to optimize production and meet regulatory standards.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Functional Fibre Optic Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Intrinsic Sensors</li><li>Extrinsic Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The functional fiber optic sensors market can be divided into two main types: intrinsic and extrinsic sensors. Intrinsic sensors utilize the fiber itself as the sensing element, detecting changes in properties like temperature or strain through variations in light within the fiber. Extrinsic sensors, on the other hand, involve external optical components that interact with the fiber, allowing for measurements of environmental factors such as pressure or chemical composition. Both types offer advantages in sensitivity, flexibility, and deployment across various applications.</p></p>

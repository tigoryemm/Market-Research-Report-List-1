<p><ul><li>"Piston Compressor"</li><li>"Screw Compressor"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In the refrigeration compressor market, semi-hermetic compressors are categorized into two main types: piston compressors and screw compressors. Piston compressors utilize reciprocating pistons to compress refrigerant, offering high efficiency and reliability, making them suitable for various applications. Screw compressors, on the other hand, employ rotating screws for continuous gas compression, delivering higher capacity and lower maintenance needs. Both types are commonly used in commercial and industrial refrigeration systems due to their effectiveness in maintaining desired temperatures.</p></p>

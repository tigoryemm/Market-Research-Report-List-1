<p><p>Macro perforated stretch film is categorized into hand film and machine film based on application and usage. Hand film is designed for manual wrapping, offering flexibility and ease of use, making it ideal for smaller operations or irregular loads. In contrast, machine film is engineered for automated systems, ensuring consistent performance and efficiency in high-volume packaging environments. Both types feature macro perforations that enhance breathability and reduce moisture buildup, making them suitable for goods that require ventilation during storage and transport.</p></p>

<p><p>The metallurgy service market encompasses various processes essential for metal extraction and processing. Sample preparation involves preparing ore samples for analysis. Comminution is the breaking down of ore into finer particles. Gravity concentration uses gravity to separate minerals based on density. Flotation employs chemicals to enhance mineral separation. Magnetic separation utilizes magnetic forces to extract ferrous materials. Hydrometallurgy involves chemical leaching to recover metals from ores. Together, these techniques optimize resource recovery and enhance metal production efficiency.</p></p>

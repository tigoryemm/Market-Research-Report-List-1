<p><p>The high temperature lubricant and grease market is divided into two main types: soap thickeners and non-soap thickeners. Soap thickeners, typically made from metallic soaps like lithium or calcium, provide good thermal stability and compatibility, making them suitable for high-temperature environments. Non-soap thickeners, such as clay or synthetic thickeners, offer enhanced performance under extreme conditions, including chemical resistance and thermal properties. Both types are crucial for applications in industries like manufacturing, automotive, and aerospace, ensuring optimal machinery performance under heat stress.</p></p>

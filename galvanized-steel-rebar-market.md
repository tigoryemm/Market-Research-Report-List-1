<p><ul><li>Conventional Hot-dip Galvanized Rebar</li><li>Continuous Hot-dip Galvanized Rebar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The galvanized steel rebar market comprises primarily two types: Conventional Hot-dip Galvanized Rebar and Continuous Hot-dip Galvanized Rebar. Conventional Hot-dip Galvanized Rebar involves immersing steel bars in molten zinc, offering corrosion resistance and enhancing durability for construction applications. On the other hand, Continuous Hot-dip Galvanized Rebar is produced in a continuous process, providing consistent quality and improved efficiency. Both types are crucial for enhancing the lifespan of reinforced concrete structures in various environmental conditions.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Castor oil-based</li><li>Glycol-based</li><li>Silicone-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The brake oil market is segmented into three main types: castor oil-based, glycol-based, and silicone-based brake fluids. Castor oil-based fluids offer a biodegradable option with good lubricating properties but can have limitations in temperature stability. Glycol-based fluids, commonly used in most vehicles, provide excellent moisture resistance and performance characteristics but can absorb water over time. Silicone-based fluids, known for their high thermal stability and non-hygroscopic nature, are often used in specialty and high-performance applications, maintaining consistent braking efficiency.</p></p>

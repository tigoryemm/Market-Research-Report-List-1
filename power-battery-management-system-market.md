<p><p>The Power Battery Management System (BMS) market comprises three key types: distributed, centralized, and modular. Distributed BMS operates across multiple battery packs, offering enhanced reliability and fault tolerance. Centralized BMS consolidates monitoring and control functions in a single unit, simplifying management but potentially reducing redundancy. Modular BMS combines aspects of both, allowing for scaling and flexibility as system requirements change. Each type caters to specific applications and performance needs, influencing their adoption across various industries.</p></p>

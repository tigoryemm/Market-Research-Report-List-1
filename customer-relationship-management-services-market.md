<p><p>Customer Relationship Management (CRM) services can be categorized into two primary types: cloud-based and on-premise. Cloud-based CRM solutions are hosted on the vendor's servers and accessed via the internet, offering flexibility, scalability, and lower upfront costs. They provide automatic updates and ease of collaboration across teams. On-premise CRM, in contrast, is installed locally on a company's own servers, allowing for greater control over data security and customization but requiring higher initial investments and ongoing maintenance.</p></p>

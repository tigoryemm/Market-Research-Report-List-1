<p><p>The stainless steel commercial kitchen knife market is divided into two main types: stamped blade products and forged blade products. Stamped blade knives are produced by cutting blades from sheets of stainless steel, resulting in lighter knives with a more affordable price point. Conversely, forged blade knives are crafted from a single piece of steel, offering superior strength, durability, and a balanced feel. Each type serves different culinary needs, appealing to both professional chefs and home cooks.</p></p>

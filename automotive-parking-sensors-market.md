<p><p>Automotive parking sensors enhance vehicle safety and ease of parking by detecting obstacles. Front parking sensors are installed in the front bumper, alerting drivers of nearby objects while maneuvering into tight spaces. They help prevent collisions with pedestrians or other vehicles. Rear parking sensors, located in the back bumper, assist when reversing, warning drivers of obstacles behind them. Both types use ultrasonic or electromagnetic technology to provide audible or visual alerts, improving overall parking accuracy and confidence.</p></p>

<p><p>The semiconductor glass wafer market includes several key types: borosilicate glass, quartz, and fused silica. Borosilicate glass is known for its thermal resistance and chemical durability, making it ideal for various semiconductor applications. Quartz offers excellent optical clarity and high thermal stability, suited for lithography and photomask technologies. Fused silica, or pure silica glass, provides superior purity and resistance to thermal shock, often utilized in high-performance semiconductor processing due to its exceptional properties. Each type serves specific industry needs.</p></p>

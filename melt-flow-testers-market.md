<p><p>Melt flow testers are categorized into three main types based on their operation: manual, semi-automatic, and automatic. Manual testers require operators to conduct all testing processes, providing control but increasing potential for human error. Semi-automatic testers streamline certain functions, reducing operator involvement while still necessitating some manual input. Automatic testers fully automate the melt flow measurement process, offering increased precision, consistency, and efficiency, making them ideal for high-throughput environments where reliability is critical.</p></p>

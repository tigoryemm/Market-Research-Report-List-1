<p><ul><li>Low Vacuum Valve</li><li>High Vacuum Valve</li><li>Ultra-high Vacuum Valve</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vacuum electromagnetic valve market comprises three main types based on vacuum levels: Low Vacuum Valves, High Vacuum Valves, and Ultra-High Vacuum Valves. Low Vacuum Valves typically operate in ranges from atmospheric pressure to 1 torr, suitable for applications like HVAC. High Vacuum Valves function from 1 torr to 10^-6 torr, crucial for industries such as semiconductor manufacturing. Ultra-High Vacuum Valves, operating below 10^-6 torr, are essential for research and industrial processes requiring extreme vacuum conditions, like particle accelerators.</p></p>

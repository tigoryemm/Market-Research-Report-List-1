<p><p>The cardiac arrhythmia monitoring devices market encompasses several types, including implantable cardiac monitors, which continuously track heart rhythms and can detect abnormal patterns over extended periods. Resting ECG devices provide quick assessments of heart activity at rest, while ECG stress test devices evaluate cardiac response during physical exertion. Mobile cardiac telemetry monitors enable real-time transmission of heart data, and Holter monitors record prolonged ECG activity over days, allowing physicians to analyze arrhythmias effectively for accurate diagnosis and treatment.</p></p>

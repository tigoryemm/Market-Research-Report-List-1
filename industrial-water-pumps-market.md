<p><ul><li>Centrifugal</li><li>Reciprocating</li><li>Rotary</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial water pumps market is primarily divided into three types: centrifugal, reciprocating, and rotary pumps. Centrifugal pumps utilize rotational energy to move water, making them ideal for continuous flow applications. Reciprocating pumps operate through a back-and-forth motion, offering precise control and high pressure, suitable for tasks requiring accuracy. Rotary pumps generate flow through the rotation of gears or vanes, providing a smooth and consistent output. Each type serves specific industrial needs, depending on flow rate, pressure, and application requirements.</p></p>

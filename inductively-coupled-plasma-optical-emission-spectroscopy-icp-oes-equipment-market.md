<p><p>Inductively Coupled Plasma Optical Emission Spectroscopy (ICP-OES) is a sophisticated analytical technique used to detect and quantify trace elements in various materials. It employs a high-temperature plasma to ionize sample constituents, followed by optical emission spectrometry to measure the emitted light, which provides information about the elemental composition.</p><p>The ICP-OES equipment market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing demand in industries such as environmental testing, food and beverage, pharmaceuticals, and metals and mining. Rising awareness of regulatory compliance and safety standards is further propelling market expansion, as more laboratories invest in advanced instruments for accurate analysis.

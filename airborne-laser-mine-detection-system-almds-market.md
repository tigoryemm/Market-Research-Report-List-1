<p><p>The Airborne Laser Mine Detection System (ALMDS) is a technology designed to detect underwater mines using advanced sensors from aerial platforms. The market for mine detection systems can be categorized into three types: </p><p>1. **Radar Based** - Utilizes electromagnetic waves to identify mines through surface reflections. </p><p>2. **Laser Based** - Employs laser beams to assess the seafloor and detect mine signatures through light reflection. </p><p>3. **Sonar Based** - Uses sound waves to create images of underwater environments, identifying mines based on acoustic signatures. Each type has its unique advantages, applications, and detection capabilities.</p></p>

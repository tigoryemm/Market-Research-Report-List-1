<p><p>High Intensity Discharge (HID) lighting encompasses several types of lamps that produce light through an electric arc between electrodes in gas-filled chambers. Xenon arc lights deliver bright, white illumination and are commonly used in projectors and automotive headlights. High-pressure sodium lights emit a warm yellow glow, ideal for street and industrial lighting due to their efficiency. Metal halide lights provide enhanced color rendering, suitable for sports arenas and retail spaces. Other types include ceramic metal halide and low-pressure sodium lamps, each tailored to specific applications.</p></p>

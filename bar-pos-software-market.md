<p><p>Bar POS software is categorized mainly into two market types: web-based and installed systems. Web-based POS solutions operate in the cloud, allowing access from any device with internet connectivity, offering flexibility and remote management features. In contrast, installed POS software is set up directly on physical hardware at the bar, ensuring reliability and speed but requiring maintenance and updates onsite. Each type serves different operational needs, with web-based systems favoring mobility and installed systems emphasizing control and stability.</p></p>

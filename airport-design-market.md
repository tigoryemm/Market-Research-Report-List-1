<p><p>Airport design encompasses two key market types: interior and exterior design. Airport interior design focuses on creating functional and aesthetically pleasing spaces within terminals, including passenger lounges, check-in areas, and retail zones. It emphasizes passenger flow, comfort, and accessibility. Conversely, airport exterior design involves the layout, architecture, and landscaping of the airport's overall structure, including runways, taxiways, and parking facilities. This aspect prioritizes operational efficiency, safety, and the first impressions of travelers, ensuring a seamless transition from outside to inside.</p></p>

UVB LEDs, emitting medium wavelengths, are essential for phototherapy and sterilization. UVC LEDs produce short wavelengths, making them highly effective for disinfection and water purification. Each type serves distinct applications across various sectors, including healthcare, environmental monitoring, and industrial processes, driving the growth and innovation within the UV LED market.</p></p>

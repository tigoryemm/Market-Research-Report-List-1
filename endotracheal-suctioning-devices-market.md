<p><p>Endotracheal suctioning devices are essential for clearing airway secretions in patients who require mechanical ventilation. The market is primarily divided into two types: closed systems and open systems. Closed systems allow suctioning while maintaining a sterile environment, reducing the risk of infection and providing convenience for healthcare professionals. In contrast, open systems involve using a separate suction catheter, which may increase the chances of contamination but offers more control during the suctioning process. Both systems cater to specific patient needs and clinical scenarios.</p></p>

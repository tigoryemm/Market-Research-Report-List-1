<p><p>The Left Ventricular Assist Device (LVAD) treatment market encompasses several types based on patient needs and treatment goals. Destination therapy involves long-term LVAD support for patients ineligible for heart transplants. Bridge to transplantation refers to using LVADs to stabilize patients awaiting heart transplants. Bridge to recovery entails temporary support while the heart heals, potentially reducing the need for transplantation. Lastly, bridge to destination signifies LVADs as a permanent solution for those not eligible for transplants or recovery.</p></p>

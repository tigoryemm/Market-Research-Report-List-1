<p><p>The seed dressing agent market is categorized into two main types: chemical dressing agents and biological dressing agents. Chemical dressing agents are synthetic products, including fungicides and insecticides, that protect seeds from pathogens and pests, enhancing germination and crop yield. Conversely, biological dressing agents are derived from natural sources, utilizing beneficial microorganisms or organic substances to promote seed health and resilience. Both types aim to improve seed viability and plant performance, catering to different agricultural practices and sustainability goals.</p></p>

<p><p>The Ti Sapphire laser market comprises three primary types: mode-locked oscillators, chirped-pulse amplifiers, and tunable continuous wave lasers. Mode-locked oscillators generate ultrashort pulses, widely used in precision measurements and medical applications. Chirped-pulse amplifiers amplify these pulses, enhancing their energy for applications in material processing and scientific research. Tunable continuous wave lasers offer adjustable wavelengths, ideal for spectroscopy and wavelength-dependent studies. Together, these technologies drive advancements in fields like telecommunications, biophotonics, and high-intensity laser applications.</p></p>

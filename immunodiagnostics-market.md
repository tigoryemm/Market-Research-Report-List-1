<p><ul><li>Immunodiagnostic Instruments</li><li>Immunoassays</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The immunodiagnostics market comprises two primary types: immunodiagnostic instruments and immunoassays. Immunodiagnostic instruments include devices such as analyzers and readers that facilitate the detection and measurement of biological substances through immune reactions. Immunoassays are laboratory techniques that utilize antibodies to detect specific antigens or biomarkers in samples, enabling disease diagnosis and monitoring. Together, these components play a crucial role in various applications, including clinical diagnostics, quality control, and research, driving advancements in disease management and therapeutic monitoring.</p></p>

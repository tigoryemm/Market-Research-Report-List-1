<p><ul><li>External Pressure</li><li>Internal Pressure</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The stator core market can be categorized into external pressure and internal pressure segments based on the stress conditions they encounter. External pressure stator cores are designed to withstand environmental forces from outside, such as fluid or atmospheric pressure, commonly used in applications like underwater or high-altitude operations. Conversely, internal pressure stator cores endure forces generated within the machine, making them ideal for high-performance motors and generators where thermal and electromagnetic stresses are significant, ensuring operational efficiency and durability.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Articulated Trucks</li><li>Rigid Trucks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The heavy mining dump truck market is primarily categorized into articulated and rigid trucks. Articulated trucks feature a flexible joint between the cab and the trailer, allowing for better maneuverability on uneven terrain, making them suitable for soft soils and complex job sites. Rigid trucks, on the other hand, have a fixed structure, offering higher payload capacities and stability on rough surfaces, making them ideal for transporting heavy loads over longer distances. Both types play crucial roles in mining operations.</p></p>

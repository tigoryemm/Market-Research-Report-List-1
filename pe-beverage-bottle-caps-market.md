<p><p>The PE Beverage Bottle Caps market primarily consists of two types: Injection Caps and Compression Mould Caps. Injection Caps are created by injecting molten plastic into a mold, offering precise shapes and intricate designs, making them suitable for high-volume production. Compression Mould Caps, on the other hand, involve compressing material within a mold, resulting in a simpler manufacturing process. Both types cater to the beverage industry, ensuring secure seals and protection against contamination while optimizing production efficiency and reducing costs.</p></p>

<p><p>The polyester matting agent market is categorized primarily into gel and powder types. Gel matting agents offer a smooth, consistent texture and are often used in coatings and paints to provide a matte finish without compromising durability. Both types serve key industries, including automotive, furniture, and textiles, enhancing aesthetic appeal and performance.</p></p>

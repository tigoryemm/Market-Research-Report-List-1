<p><p>The household water purifier market is primarily categorized into three types: RO (Reverse Osmosis) water purifiers, UV (Ultraviolet) water purifiers, and others. RO purifiers use a semi-permeable membrane to eliminate impurities and dissolved solids, making water safe for consumption. UV purifiers utilize ultraviolet light to disinfect water by killing harmful microorganisms, ensuring it is free from pathogens. Other types include carbon filters and gravity-based systems, addressing varied consumer needs and preferences for water purification solutions.</p></p>
